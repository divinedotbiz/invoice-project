## 📄 Creating Your First Invoice

### Step 1: Company Information

Fill out **your company details** (this appears on every invoice):

- **Company Name**: Your business name
- **Address**: Full business address
- **Email**: Your contact email
- **Phone**: Your phone number
- **Logo** (optional): Upload a PNG/JPG logo

### Step 2: Invoice Details

Set the **invoice specifics**:

- **Invoice #**: Auto-generated (INV-2024-001, etc.)
- **Issue Date**: When you sent the invoice
- **Due Date**: When payment is due (auto: 30 days out)

### Step 3: Billing To (Client)

Enter **who you're invoicing**:

- **Client Name**: Your customer's name
- **Address**: Their address
- **Email**: Their email
- **Phone**: Their phone number

**💡 Tip**: Use "Choose Client" button to quickly select a saved client.

### Step 4: Line Items

Add **what you're charging for**:

1. Click **"Add Line Item"**
2. Enter:
   - **Description**: What the client is paying for
   - **Quantity**: How many units
   - **Unit Price**: Price per unit
   - **Tax %**: Tax rate (0% if no tax)
3. **Total auto-calculates** ✨

**Add multiple items** by clicking "Add Line Item" again.

### Step 5: Totals

Review your **invoice totals**:

- **Subtotal**: Sum of all items (before tax)
- **Tax**: Total tax across all items
- **Grand Total**: Final amount due

*These update automatically as you edit!*

### Step 6: Notes & Terms

Add **optional payment instructions**:

- Payment terms ("Net 30", "Due upon receipt", etc.)
- Thank you message
- Special instructions for the client

## 👥 Managing Clients

### Add a New Client

1. Click **"Clients"** button (header)
2. Click **"+ Add Client"**
3. Enter:
   - Client name (required)
   - Email
   - Address
   - Phone
4. Click **"Save Client"**

**Now** this client appears in your "Choose Client" dropdown!

### Edit a Client

1. Click **"Clients"**
2. Find the client in the list
3. Click **"Edit"** (pencil icon)
4. Update their info
5. Click **"Update"**

### Delete a Client

1. Click **"Clients"**
2. Find the client in the list
3. Click **"Delete"** (trash icon)
4. Confirm deletion

### Reuse Clients

Once saved, quickly add a client to an invoice:

1. Click **"Choose Client"** button (Bill To section)
2. Click the client name
3. ✨ All their info auto-fills!

## 📥 Downloading & Sharing

### Download as PDF

1. Complete your invoice
2. Click **"Download PDF"** button (top right or sidebar)
3. File downloads immediately as `invoice-[number].pdf`
4. Attach to email and send to your client

**PDF Includes**:
- Your company logo (if uploaded)
- All invoice details
- Line items and calculations
- Professional formatting
- Ready to send!

### Print Invoice

1. Complete your invoice
2. Press **Ctrl+P** (Windows) or **Cmd+P** (Mac)
3. Browser print dialog opens
4. Select printer and print

**Clean print layout** — no buttons, clean design, optimal for paper.

## 📊 Tracking Invoices in History

### View All Invoices

1. Click **"History"** button (header)
2. See a table of all your invoices

**Columns show**:
- Invoice number
- Client name
- Amount
- Payment status
- Issue & due dates

### Update Invoice Status

As you work with clients, update the status:

- 📝 **Draft** — Not yet sent
- ✉️ **Sent** — Email sent to client
- 👁️ **Viewed** — Client opened email
- ✅ **Paid** — Payment received
- ⚠️ **Overdue** — Past due date
- ❌ **Cancelled** — Invoice cancelled

**To update**: Go back to the invoice and change the status dropdown.

### Edit an Invoice

From History:

1. Click the **pencil icon** 🖊️
2. You're taken back to the form
3. Make changes
4. Changes auto-save

### Duplicate an Invoice

Quickly create a similar invoice:

1. Click the **duplicate icon** 📋
2. New invoice opens with same items
3. Update client, dates, amounts
4. Ready to download!

### Delete an Invoice

1. Click the **trash icon** 🗑️
2. Confirm deletion
3. Invoice removed (can't undo)

### Search & Filter

Find invoices quickly:

- **Search box**: Type invoice # or client name
- **Status filter**: Select a status to show only those
- **See results**: Table updates instantly

## ❓ FAQ

### Q: Where is my data stored?
**A**: On your computer/device in browser storage. Not on any server. Only YOU can access it.

### Q: Can I use this offline?
**A**: Yes! Create invoices without internet. Data syncs when online.

### Q: Can multiple people use this?
**A**: Yes, but each person needs their own browser/device. Data is per-device.

### Q: How do I backup my invoices?
**A**: Export invoices from History, or save PDFs of important invoices.

### Q: Is my data secure?
**A**: Yes! Everything stays on your device. No uploads to servers.

### Q: Can I access my invoices from another computer?
**A**: No. Data is stored locally. Use the same computer for consistency.

### Q: What if I clear my browser data?
**A**: Invoices will be deleted. Save important PDFs before clearing.

### Q: Can I edit an old invoice?
**A**: Yes! Go to History, click the edit icon, make changes, and save.

### Q: Does the PDF include my logo?
**A**: Yes! If you upload a logo, it appears on the PDF invoice.

### Q: How many invoices can I create?
**A**: As many as you want (limited by browser storage, typically 5-50MB).

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting

### PDF Download Not Working

**Try**:
1. Use a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge)
2. Check that ad blockers aren't blocking downloads
3. Check your Downloads folder
4. Try printing to PDF instead (Ctrl+P → "Save as PDF")

### Data Not Saving

**Try**:
1. Check you're not in Incognito/Private mode
2. Reload the page and try again
3. Check browser storage isn't full
4. Try a different browser

### Fonts Look Wrong

**Try**:
1. Reload the page (Ctrl+R / Cmd+R)
2. Clear browser cache
3. Works offline too (just looks slightly different)

### Prices Not Calculating

**Try**:
1. Make sure you entered numbers (not text)
2. Delete and re-enter the amount
3. Reload the page
4. Try a different browser

### Page Won't Load

**Try**:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Clear browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete)
3. Try a different browser
4. Restart your computer
